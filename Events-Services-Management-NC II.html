Events and Services Management NC II

The Events and Services Management NC II program is designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge necessary to plan, organize, and manage various events and services. This program covers a wide range of topics including event planning, customer service, marketing, and financial management.

Students will learn how to create event proposals, manage budgets, coordinate logistics, and ensure customer satisfaction. The program also emphasizes the importance of effective communication and teamwork in the successful execution of events.

Upon completion of this program, graduates will be prepared to work in various roles within the events industry, including event coordinators, service managers, and customer service representatives.

Graduates of Events Management Services NC III are competent to be:

  • Events Coordinator
  • Conference Coordinator
  • Function Coordinator

Trainee Entry Requirements

Applicants should meet the following:

  • Can communicate in basic English in both oral and written form
  • Physically and mentally fit
  • With good moral character
  • Can perform basic mathematical computation
