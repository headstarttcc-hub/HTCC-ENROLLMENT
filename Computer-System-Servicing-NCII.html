Computer System Servicing NC II

This course is designed to develop & enhance the knowledge, skills, & attitudes of a Computer Systems Service Technician, in accordance with industry standards. It covers the basic and common competencies including installing and configuring computer systems, setting up computer networks and servers, and maintaining or repairing systems and networks.

Students will gain hands-on experience and technical expertise in hardware, software, diagnostics, and network configurations—making them job-ready for today’s digital environment.

Career Opportunities:

  • Computer Assembler
  • Computer Service Technician
  • Network Technician
  • Computer Maintenance Technician
