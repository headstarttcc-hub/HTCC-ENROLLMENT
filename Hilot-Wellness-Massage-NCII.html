Hilot (Wellness Massage) NC II

The Hilot (Wellness Massage) NC II program is designed to develop and enhance the knowledge, skills, and attitudes of individuals in accordance with wellness industry standards. It includes the delivery of wellness massage techniques rooted in traditional Filipino healing practices.

Students will learn various massage methods, proper hygiene and safety protocols, client assessment, preparation of massage tools and equipment, and techniques that promote relaxation, healing, and overall well-being.

Upon completion, graduates are equipped to deliver quality wellness massage services either in wellness centers, spas, or as independent practitioners.

Trainee Entry Requirements

Applicants for HILOT (WELLNESS MASSAGE) NC II should meet the following:

  • 18 years old and above
  • Must pass the personality/aptitude test
  • Can communicate effectively in oral and written form
  • Physically, emotionally, and mentally fit
  • Of good moral character
